## Purpose

Make schedules and delivery bundles release artifacts, not loose files. v5.2
hardens export/compare/package traceability after v5.0 RC and v5.1 sheet
control evidence. This plan can proceed as a schedule/package-only portable
hardening lane while live Revit schedule smoke remains explicitly gated.

## Commands

- `schedules batch-export`
- `schedules compare`
- `deliverables verify`
- `deliverables bundle`
- `deliverables plan`
- `issue package`
- `journal verify`
